SEVENTEEN: The Shining Diamond of the Kpop Industry

SEVENTEEN is a KPOP group under the company PLEDIS, which is now currently under HYBE. They debuted on May 26, 2015, and are now one of the biggest groups, reaching some of the same achievements as BTS.
Their group name symbolizes the sum of their members (13), the units (3), and their group as a whole (1). Their fans are named Carats, and their official colors are serenity and rose quartz.

Reasons Why I Stan/Like SEVENTEEN

  • Self-made: Their producer, Woozi, is also the leader of the Vocal unit, and they all participate in creating the lyrics.
  • Comfort place: As they have been together for ten years, they naturally act as a family and also show their comedic side comfortably in their content.
  • Talent: Training at a young age helped them with their synchronization, as well as their ability to sing through hard choreographies while still having fun on stage.
